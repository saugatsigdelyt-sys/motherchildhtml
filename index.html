Configuration

⚠️ Your Meta App must have completed App Review for whatsapp_business_management and whatsapp_business_messaging permissions, and must be registered as a Tech Provider or Solution Partner.
How this works:
1. Fill in your Backend URL, App ID, App Secret, and Configuration ID.
2. Click Connect WhatsApp Business — the Embedded Signup popup launches.
3. Complete the Meta login flow to select/create a WhatsApp Business Account.
4. On completion, your backend exchanges the code for an access token and saves everything to Supabase.
5. The saved account (with token) can be used by any Cloud API-compatible webhook integration.
